Halloumi Burger

Savor the excellence of our Halloumi Burger – a vegetarian masterpiece for all burger enthusiasts! Grilled or pan-fried Halloumi cheese, nestled in a soft bun, meets a medley of fresh veggies and tantalizing condiments. A symphony of salty and savory, it's the perfect meatless delight to elevate your dining experience. Bite into bliss with our Halloumi Burger – a journey of extraordinary flavors!

Buffalo Wings

Elevate your taste buds with our sensational Buffalo Wings at Nolita Joe's! Immerse yourself in a world of bold and spicy flavors, where crispy, succulent chicken wings meet the fiery kick of our signature buffalo sauce. Each bite is a symphony of tangy, savory, and just the right amount of heat that'll leave you craving more.

Alfredo Pasta

Indulge in the exquisite delight of our signature Alfredo Pasta – a culinary masterpiece that promises a symphony of rich flavors and luxurious textures. Picture perfectly cooked fettuccine noodles generously embraced by our velvety blend of butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. Each bite is a journey into pure satisfaction, delivering an unparalleled experience that elevates comfort food to new heights.
